OEM Partnership Programs

WB Distributing partners with Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) to deliver premium aftermarket solutions that empower dealers and enhance customer satisfaction. Through strategic alliances like our Orange Aftermarket program with Kubota Tractor Corporation, we provide seamless distribution, innovative marketing support, and premium product offerings.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Access a single source for high-quality aftermarket attachments and accessories.
  • Extend your dealers’ product offerings with non-competing premium solutions.
  • Leverage our nationwide distribution network for fast and reliable service.
  • Enhance your brand presence with our participation in dealer meetings and events.

Our programs are designed to align with your goals, helping you build stronger relationships with dealers while meeting the needs of customers across the country.
